Welcome!

Hello everyone! I'm Dani, a Software Engineer with a passion for Cybersecurity based in Copenhagen. I'm currently pursuing my Master's Degree in Defensive and Offensive Cybersecurity while I study to get the Offsec Pen-200 certification. Since I spend a few hours a day learning and practicing, I have decided to share what I do through this website. Writing things down helps me stay organized and work more efficiently. Sometimes it’s hard to recall how to exploit a specific service or how a script works, so this space will serve as a reference for myself and, hopefully, for anyone on a similar path. In the Learning Journey section, I’ll post quick updates about what I’ve been working on and learning. You’ll also find Writeups for the CTFs and challenges I complete. If you want to connect with me, feel free to add me on LinkedIn!
